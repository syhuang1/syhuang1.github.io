This paper studies sampling-efficient test-time scaling for large language models and proposes Self-Truncation Best-of-N (ST-BoN), which estimates promising samples during early decoding to reduce the cost of best-of-N sampling.

Accepted by NeurIPS 2025 as a Spotlight.
