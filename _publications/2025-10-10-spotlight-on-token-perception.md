This paper studies multimodal reinforcement learning from the perspective of token-level visual perception and proposes Visually-Perceptive Policy Optimization (VPPO), a policy gradient algorithm that focuses learning signals on visually grounded trajectories and tokens.

Accepted by ICLR 2026.
